Project Summary:

This project is about tracking down the disbursement of SBA Loans between 2012 and 2020 in the United States. The data was sourced from the AWS public database by my teammate James Smith of J2P2 (James, Jessica, Pariya, and Pius) project team. This project will use a number tools to answer the following business questions namely:-

-->
1. What is the total borrowing for each distict office?
2. What is the total amount of loans issued by State?
3. What are the top 10 banks that issued the highest loans?
4. What are the top 20 States where borrowers are located?
5. What types of businesses received the loans?
6. Which methods were used to deliver the loans?
7. What are the average initial interest rate for the loans?
8. What is average loan term limits in months?
9. What are the loan status (CANCLD,CHGOFF, EXEMPT, PIF)?
10. What jobs were supported by the loans?
